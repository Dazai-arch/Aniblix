About Us

We are passionate anime enthusiasts who realized the need for a dedicated platform to track anime progress and facilitate booking anime movie tickets.

Our Story

Our journey began with our love for anime and the frustration of missing out on episodes or screenings due to a lack of organized information. Determined to solve this problem, we embarked on a mission to create a platform that would not only track anime series progress but also connect fans with local screenings and events.
